PREMISES

The subject of sale is the area called Kloster Schwalmtal - Hostert with a complex of buildings erected in 1912-1916.

The total area of the site is approximately 100 800 m2.

The new architectural design envisages enlarging the outward extension area from 30,000 to 100,000 m2.

The plot is equipped with all utilities: electricity, water and sewage.

Local authorities are open to any proposals to develop this beautiful place.

If you purchase the plot of 50,000 m2, where buildings are located, you can deduct 90% from tax for 10 years.

PRELIMINARY VISUALIZATION

The following buildings can be built on the presented area (provided that their height not exceeding the last floor of the monastery is preserved):

  • a 4-5 star hotel of approx. 1,000 rooms with restaurants, conference rooms and a SPA area; the hotel can act as an independent investment due to separate utilities connection.
  • a clinic for approx. 85 rooms with full treatment and rehabilitation facilities that can operate independently due to separate utilities connection.
  • trade and exhibition complex with an area of 30 000 m2
  • restaurant with concert hall with VIP rooms
  • 2 - 3 star hotel for tour groups

All facilities have additional spaces for car parks and recreation areas.

CURRENT STATE

There are the following buildings on the offered plot:

  • a former church with stained-glass windows, a monastery and an administrative building, preliminary designs assume converting it into a restaurant, concert hall and hotel rooms
  • the so-called School - two large buildings that once housed 6.000 students, their interiors require a complete renovation, talking into account the preserved details in the neo-Gothic style.

The buildings are surrounded by a park with marked out avenues.
